Internet


Are you up to speed?

In a time when the internet is connected to innovation, job growth, working from home and education, internet accessibility is essential.
However, analyses from the Pew Research Center and other studies indicate that a digital divide exists in the U.S.
With around 10 percent of Americans without internet access and others facing gaps in internet speed,
consistently unreliable internet, among other problems.

This project is meant to determine the factors that play a role in internet connectivity within a community using machine learning. The project's goal is to use the analysis to inform a model that can help communities determine the best
approaches to bringing more people online at higher download speeds. The ineteractive dashboard will allows users increase internet coverage by targeting the factors identified with the analysis.

To learn more, please visit our Google Slides Presentation displaying the workflow, project parameters and analysis among other related topics.
